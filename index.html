    Biography

    I am a research fellow in the Department of Sociology and Economic Law at University of Bologna.

    My research interests lie in applied microeconomics, with a focus in labour and cultural economics. I intend to understand how various socio-economic factors influence individual perceptions and behaviors, ultimately affecting the labor market.
